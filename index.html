About Nirmal

Runner · Cyclist · Banker (15+ yrs)

Nirmal Lamsal loves to run and cycle. He has already run 100,000 KM. He has participated in extreme marathons such as the Peyong Frozen Lake Marathon — ascending from sea level to 4000 meters. He is an avid cyclist and currently training for multiple ultra-marathons while balancing a banking career spanning over 15 years.
