About Me

Nirmal Lamsal loves to run and cycle. He has already run over 100,000 KM.

He has participated in extreme marathons like the Peyong Frozen Lake Marathon, running from sea level to 4000 meters altitude.

He is a passionate cyclist and is currently training for ultra-marathons.

Professionally, he is a banker with over 15 years of experience.
